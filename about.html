About The Little Akara Shop

The Little Akara Shop is an all-day breakfast cafe that offers "Mixed Nigerian Breakfast", a mix of Nigerian and foreign breakfast basics - with Akara as staple, to city-dwellers seeking easy, affordable meal options; and guests seeking a nostalgic/reminiscent breakfast experience. It was founded in 2022. Our shop was built from a love for akara. We hope our shop adds a unique and interesting place to our little town.

Akara Trivia

When did akara first appear in Nigeria?

Akara, a popular Nigerian bean cake, likely emerged in Nigeria before the transatlantic slave trade. Whike akara's origins are rooted in West Africa, its spread to Brazil and some parts of the Americas is strongly linked to the forced migration of enslaved Yoruba people. (Source: Wikipedia)
